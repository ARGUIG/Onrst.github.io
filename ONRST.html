    About Us:

    ONRST is a private nonprofit association based in Rabat, Morocco, focused on fostering R&D in ICT, Energy, and Industrial Management.

    It collaborates with several universities and institutions through the Trusted Cluster Scientific Researchers network, working on R&D projects funded by industry under Moroccan R&D programs. Its vision aligns with King Mohamed VI's Strategy for the Evolution of National Scientific Research, aiming to promote applied scientific research for economic transformation and social inclusion.

    The Observatory brings together teacher-researchers, doctoral engineers, and students for multidisciplinary research projects, specializing in areas such as digitization engineering, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and more.
